## Engagement Models

### Project-Based Consulting
- Defined scope and deliverables
- Fixed timeline and budget
- Clear success criteria and measurement
- Ideal for specific, well-defined challenges
- Typical duration: 2-6 months

### Retainer Consulting
- Ongoing advisory relationship
- Flexible scope and priorities
- Regular check-ins and consultation
- Ideal for complex, evolving challenges
- Typical duration: 6-24 months

### Embedded Consulting
- Consultant integrated into organization
- Full-time or part-time presence
- Active participation in decision-making
- Ideal for crisis response or transformation
- Typical duration: 3-12 months

### Advisory Board Participation
- Strategic guidance at board level
- Periodic meetings and consultation
- External perspective and expertise
- Ideal for ongoing strategic challenges
- Typical duration: 1-3 years
