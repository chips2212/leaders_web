# Mathieu Dufour

## Coach for Impact & Organizational Development Practitioner

Mathieu Dufour is a humanitarian operative and coach with over a decade of experience leading high-performance teams in extreme field conditions across 40 countries. A battle-tested manager who thrives under pressure, he has evolved from being "in the trenches" to helping others navigate rapid change and gain clarity amidst uncertainty.

### Humanitarian Leadership Experience

Mathieu has held senior leadership positions in major humanitarian organizations:

- **Director of Finances** at MSF Barcelona (2018-2023): Top management of a 6000+ employee organization, leading a finance team of 20+ people with an annual budget of 200M€ and worldwide multi-year financial negotiations for 8Bn€ agreements
- **Chief Financial Officer** at ALIMA (2013-2018): Transition management from a 12M to 35M€ startup, successful management of a 2M€ social bond issuance, business planning on 10-year projections
- **Operational Finance** at MSF (2010-2013): Multiple international assignments in sensitive environments including:
  - Administrative setup of a 230-bed hospital post-earthquake in Haiti
  - Financial coordination in conflict zones in DRC (North Kivu)
  - Support missions and coaching teams in Chechnya, Colombia, Madagascar, Burkina Faso

### Coaching & Development Work

Since 2021, Mathieu has been coaching around emotions and connections:

- **Professional Coach - Chief Emotional Officer** at "Listened to like never before"
- **Associate Certified Coach (ACC)** with International Coaching Federation (ICF)
- **EMCC Senior Practitioner (EIA)**
- **Organizational Development Practitioner** at European Climate Foundation (2024-present), supporting partner organizations working toward Paris Agreement goals

### Coaching Approach

Mathieu creates quality spaces through trust and active listening, facilitating pivotal moments to inspire action and engagement. He specializes in:

- Executive and personal growth
- Interpersonal relationships
- Time management
- Organizational leadership development
- Eco-anxiety and climate-related concerns
