# Omar Hayek

## HR Professional & Leadership Coach

Omar Hayek is a dynamic Coach, Trainer, and HR Professional with over 10 years of experience in education consulting, quality management, and entrepreneurship. With expertise in staff development, coaching, mentoring, and training, he has a strong emphasis on employee relations and team building in multicultural environments.

### Humanitarian & Development Experience

Omar has established HR functions and coached teams across multiple humanitarian organizations:

- **Regional Mentoring & Coaching Officer** at MSF Dakar (2023), responsible for developing actions to support professional development and implementing the Mentoring & Coaching offer in MSF operations
- **Individual & Team Coach** at MSF Norway (2020-present), providing online coaching sessions supporting MSF employees globally and improving team dynamics through team coaching
- **Mission Human Resources Manager** at MSF France in Beqaa, Lebanon (2017-2020), establishing an HR department from the ground up and recruiting diverse talent
- **Human Resources Manager** at Union Relief & Development Agency in Beirut (2015-2017), developing and implementing effective HR department and performance management systems
- **Emergency Team Leader** at International Organization for Migration in Lebanon (2013-2014), coordinating cross-sectoral activities and overseeing distribution programs

### Training & Facilitation

- **Ways of Working Trainer** at MSF New York Office (2020-2022), implementing new training modules to improve effectiveness and efficiency
- **Employment Hub Trainer & Coach** at NAWAYA Organization Lebanon (2022), providing training to enhance soft skills and employability for Lebanese youth
- **Train of Trainer** certification from MSF USA, New York (2018)
