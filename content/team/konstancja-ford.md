# Konstancja Ford (Konni)

## Organizational Development Specialist & Team Coach

Konstancja Ford is an Organizational Development Specialist with over 20 years of experience in leadership, learning, and culture in complex international and public sector contexts. As an EMCC Senior Practitioner Coach, she has successfully developed and managed HR, learning and development, and back-office administration at ING and Médecins Sans Frontières.

### MSF & Humanitarian Experience

Since 2013, Konni has worked extensively with MSF:

- **Organizational Development & Team Coaching Program Manager** (2022-Present) at MSF International
- **Human Resources Development Specialist/Referent** (2019-2022) at MSF Belgium, Brussels
- **Capacity Building Mobile Officer** (2017-2019) specializing in Management & Leadership development
- **Nordic Training and Development Manager** (2013-2015), analyzing L&D needs and developing concepts for fieldworkers from Norway, Sweden, and Denmark
- **Family Support Principal in Crisis Management Team**, supporting staff and families during critical incidents

### Coaching & Consulting Work

As Partner at Rainmen (2017-present), Konni provides:

- **International Team Coaching** for multinational teams in challenging global environments (Bangladesh, Uganda, Tajikistan, South Africa, Kenya, Somalia)
- **Organizational Development Consulting** for HR strategy and operational implementation
- **Strategy Execution** supporting organizations through transformation and change

### Previous Experience

- **Head of Learning & Development** at MSF Amsterdam (2015-2017), establishing an international L&D department (20 FTE)
- **Head of Human Resource Management** at MSF Amsterdam (2012-2015), HR responsibility for 30 countries
- **Various HR Leadership Roles** at ING Group Amsterdam (1997-2012), including HR transformation and organizational restructuring

### Credentials

- EMCC/NOBCO Senior Practitioner Coach Accreditation (2021)
- Systemic Team & Organizational Coaching (De Bewustzijnsfabriek, 2024)
- Organizational & Relationship Systems Coaching (CTI, 2020)
- Profile Dynamics Certified (2022)
- Master in HR Development & Adult Education (Roskilde University)
- Master of Science in Anthropology (Københavns Universitet)
