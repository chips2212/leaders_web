# Luis Encinas Pedrayes

## Coach-Mentor & Humanitarian Operations Specialist

Luis Encinas Pedrayes brings a unique combination of medical expertise and humanitarian leadership experience. With a background in Nursing (1992) and Tropical Medicine (1994), he has spent nearly three decades in the humanitarian field with Médecins Sans Frontières, occupying different positions in the field and at headquarters, including 8 years as Operational Coordinator.

### Humanitarian Crisis Experience

Luis has led medical and operational teams across the globe:

- **Operational Coordinator** (8 years) overseeing multiple complex emergencies
- **Head of Mission** and **Medical Coordinator** in diverse humanitarian contexts:
  - Yemen
  - Democratic Republic of Congo
  - Chad
  - Haiti
  - Colombia
  - Occupied Palestinian Territory
  - Cambodia
  - Afghanistan
- **Field Nursing Activities Management** in emergency settings

### Coaching Expertise

Since 2016, Luis has developed his coaching practice with a specific focus on group coaching:

- **Individual & Group Coaching Sessions** for humanitarian professionals
- **250+ coaching hours** dedicated to coaching, both online and face-to-face
- **MSF Norway Coaching Certificate** with EIA Accreditation (2018-2019)
- Specialization in supporting humanitarian workers through career transitions and leadership challenges

### Medical Background

- **Intensive Care, Emergency & Psychiatric Care** with Servicio Canario de Salud (2021-present)
- **Primary Health Care** managing diabetic patients
- **Emergency Response** in post-earthquake Haiti, setting up a 230-bed hospital
- **Financial Coordination** in conflict zones (North Kivu, DRC)
