# Courtney Mather

## Executive Coach & Leadership Development Specialist

Courtney Mather is an executive coach with 10+ years of experience coaching leaders at all levels—from emerging leaders to C-suite and board members—across the globe. Recognized for her ability to connect with individuals and earn their trust in diverse cultural contexts, she brings a unique blend of corporate and humanitarian leadership development expertise.

### Humanitarian Crisis Experience

Courtney's field-tested crisis leadership includes:

- **Individual Coaching Program Manager** at Doctors Without Borders (MSF) Norway, maintaining an internal pool of coaches and responding to acute coaching needs during the COVID-19 pandemic
- **Family Focal Point** during crisis incidents in South Sudan, offering psychosocial support to families of affected staff and facilitating workshops for impacted teams
- **Flying Human Resources Coach** across 8 countries (South Sudan, Central African Republic, Niger, Kenya, Sudan, Tanzania, Mexico/Honduras, Kyrgyzstan), coaching HR leaders and senior leadership through team conflicts
- **Human Resources Coordinator** in Juba, South Sudan, leading HR operations in active conflict zones

### Corporate & Development Experience

- **Global Induction Project Manager** at MSF Geneva (6.9 million CHF budget), developing innovative blended learning programs
- **Associate Director** at Starwood Hotels & Resorts, directing $15M training development for global technological projects
- **Field Director** with WorldTeach in Colombia, establishing operations and coaching volunteer teachers in remote locations

### Coaching Specializations

- Executive and leadership coaching for high-stakes environments
- 360° leadership assessments and stakeholder feedback synthesis
- Crisis leadership and psychosocial support
- Team dynamics and conflict resolution
- Strengths-based coaching (Gallup Certified)

### Credentials

- EMCC Global Accredited Coach (EIA) Practitioner Level
- Gallup-Certified Strengths Coach
- Lead Out of the Box Leadership (ICF-Accredited)
- Master of Arts, Counseling Psychology (Pacifica Graduate Institute)
- Certified Scrum Product Owner®
