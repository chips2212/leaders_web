# Dr. Amani Almashaqbeh

## Crisis Leadership Coach & Mental Health Specialist

Dr. Amani Almashaqbeh is an EMCC-trained coach and psychologist with over 15 years of experience in international humanitarian and emergency contexts. With a PhD in Psychology and advanced training in family and couples therapy, she has led large-scale Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS), protection, and Gender-Based Violence (GBV) programs across the Middle East, Africa, and Eastern Europe.

### Field Experience

Amani's crisis leadership experience includes:

- **Mental Health Activities Manager** with Médecins Sans Frontières in Iraq (Mosul), Lebanon (Beirut, Bekaa), and Sudan (Darfur)
- **MHPSS & Protection Program Manager** with Action Against Hunger in Ukraine (Dnipro, Kharkiv, Zaporizhzhia)
- **Individual Coaching** with MSF Norway supporting staff well-being and leadership development
- **Emergency Response** managing mobile medical units for displaced persons in Lebanon

### Coaching Approach

Amani's coaching and training work are deeply informed by her clinical background and leadership in volatile and high-pressure humanitarian settings. She specializes in supporting health and humanitarian professionals to:

- Build resilience and prevent burnout
- Strengthen leadership skills in crisis contexts
- Navigate complex ethical decisions under pressure
- Find renewed purpose in challenging environments

### Credentials

- PhD in Psychology, Counselling, and Psychological Health (Jordan University)
- Master in Family and Couples Psychotherapy (Hashemite University)
- Diploma in NGO Management (German Jordanian University)
- EMCC-trained Coach

### Geographic Experience

Iraq, Lebanon, Sudan, Ukraine (East and West), Myanmar, Jordan, Syria
