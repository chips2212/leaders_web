# Loïc Jaeger

## Crisis Leadership Coach & Strategic Consultant

Loïc Jaeger brings 25 years of professional experience, including 15 years leading teams in international crisis contexts and 5 years empowering leaders through coaching, mentoring, training, and strategic consulting. As an EMCC EIA Senior Practitioner, he combines deep field experience with advanced coaching credentials.

### Crisis Leadership Experience

Loïc has led missions in some of the world's most challenging environments:

- **Executive Director** for MSF Mexico and Central America (2019-2023), providing visionary leadership and creating the integrated CAMINO office
- **Head of Mission** in various crises including Syria, Ukraine, South Sudan, Afghanistan, and Greece (forced displacement)
- **Field Coordinator** in conflicts in Pakistan, Colombia, and forced displacement in Liberia
- **Senior Project Leader for OD Americas**, developing community-centric operational management models

### Coaching & Development Work

Since 2018, Loïc has provided:

- **International Coach and Mentor** (EMCC EIA Senior Practitioner level), partnering with executives to enhance leadership presence and navigate complex ethical decisions
- **Trainer specialized in leadership courses**, facilitating training for humanitarian organizations
- **Strategic Consultant** accompanying NGOs in multi-year strategy development

### Key Achievements

- Successfully negotiated MSF's presence and access to vulnerable populations in challenging security environments
- Merged institutional and operational teams, creating the CAMINO office—a global MSF precedent
- Increased private fundraising from 6 to 20 million Mexican pesos in four years
- Global Executive Sponsor for DEI, initiating projects addressing structural racism
- Developed Critical Incident Management plans and responded to multiple security incidents

### Credentials

- EMCC EIA Certification at Senior Practitioner level (2021)
- IQTS for Sexual Exploitation, Abuse and Harassment Investigations (2024)
- Training on Evaluation of Humanitarian Programs (2018)
- MSF Coaching Education (ICF ACSTH program, 2018)
- MSF Mentoring Workshop (EMCC ISMCP Gold Level, 2016)

### Geographic Experience

Syria, Ukraine, South Sudan, Afghanistan, Greece, Pakistan, Colombia, Liberia, Mexico, Central America
