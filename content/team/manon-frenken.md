# Manon Frenken

## Team Coach & Organizational Development Consultant

Manon Frenken is a driven, analytical, and result-oriented professional with over 20 years of experience helping teams, organizations, and leaders successfully navigate transformations. Her expertise spans international leadership roles at ING and Médecins Sans Frontières, where she developed and managed HR, learning and development, and back-office administration.

### MSF & Humanitarian Experience

- **International Team Coach** (2017-present): Coaching multinational teams in challenging global environments including Bangladesh, Uganda, Tajikistan, South Africa, Kenya, and Somalia
- **Consultant Organizational Development** for MSF Operational Centre Switzerland, developing and implementing strategic HR policies
- **Head of Learning & Development** at MSF Amsterdam (2015-2017): Established and managed an international L&D department (20 FTE), developed management programmes, and improved workflow processes
- **Head of Human Resource Management** at MSF Amsterdam (2012-2015): HR responsibility for 30 countries, managing a team of 50 FTE, implementing decentralized HR functions

### Coaching & Consulting Work

As Partner at Rainmen (2017-present), Manon delivers:

- **Organizational Development and Strategy Execution** supporting organizations through transformation
- **Team Coaching** using systemic and relationship systems approaches
- **Interim Management** for HR and operational functions
- **Crisis Management and Executive Coaching** restoring trust and clarifying roles during difficult transitions

### Key Projects

- Global People Strategy for Norwegian Refugee Council—new leadership profiles and modernization of performance management
- Transformation Lead for RNW Media—reorganization guidance and new structure development
- HR Manager/Coach for AT5—crisis management and executive coaching
