# Ina'm Shakhatreh

## Strategic Humanitarian Operations Leader & Certified Leadership Coach

Ina'm Shakhatreh is a dynamic humanitarian leader with over 10 years of global experience in emergency response, strategic coordination, and leadership coaching across conflict zones and international missions. Her proven track record includes negotiating access, managing complex operations, and developing staff capacities in multicultural environments.

### Crisis Leadership Experience

Ina'm has led through some of the world's most challenging humanitarian contexts:

- **Head of Mission Support** with MSF in Iraq (Dohuk and Iraq missions), leading emergency response training and security strategy development
- **Project Coordinator** in Mosul, Iraq, supervising emergency programs and coordinating with multiple MSF sections
- **Cell 5 Executive Assistant (Ops + HR)** at MSF Amman, managing complex executive coordination across departments
- **Trainer and Facilitator for Negotiations** with the Center of Competence in Humanitarian Negotiations (CCHN) in Geneva

### Coaching & Development Work

Since 2019, Ina'm has provided:

- **Life and Leadership Coaching** for INGOs including MSF and international institutions
- **Workshop Facilitation** designing and leading Coaching Skills Workshops to enhance leadership capabilities and emotional intelligence
- **Personalized Coaching & Mentorship** delivering one-on-one and team coaching for managers and supervisors
- **Resilience & Mindset Cultivation** guiding leaders in overcoming challenges and developing growth-oriented mindsets

### Key Expertise

- Project & Program Management in volatile contexts
- Risk & Security Assessment
- Negotiation & Mediation with state and non-state actors
- Cross-Cultural Communication
- Proposal & Grant Writing
- Interpretation & Translation (Arabic/English)
